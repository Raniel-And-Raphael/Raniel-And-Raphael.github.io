Worship

Worship of God is the act of honoring and praising Him for who he is and what he has done for us. Worshiping involves expressing our love, gratitude, adoration and devotion to God through various forms such as prayer, singing, reading the Bible, giving, serving and obeying.

Gospel preaching

Preaching gospel is the act of proclaiming the good news of Jesus Christ to the world. Preaching gospel is a command and a commission given by Jesus to his followers before he ascended to heaven.
